Mileage Tracker — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Overview

Mileage Tracker is a personal mileage logging app designed to help you track business, medical, and charitable trips for IRS tax deduction purposes. Your privacy is important to us.

Data We Collect

Location Data: With your permission, Mileage Tracker accesses your device's GPS location to automatically track trips and calculate mileage. Location data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

Trip Information: Trip dates, mileage, categories, notes, and vehicle information are stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework.

Motion & Fitness Data: With your permission, the app uses motion activity to detect when you are driving, enabling automatic trip detection.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect any personal identification information
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not use advertising

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud, Apple may sync your data across your devices through your personal iCloud account, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Permissions

  • Location (Always): Required for automatic trip detection and mileage calculation. You can disable this in iOS Settings at any time.
  • Motion & Fitness: Used to detect driving activity for automatic trip start. Optional.
  • Bluetooth: Used to detect connection to vehicle Bluetooth for automatic trip start. Optional.
  • Notifications: Used to alert you when a trip is complete and for smart route suggestions. Optional.
  • Camera & Photo Library: Used for attaching receipt photos to trips. Optional.

Children's Privacy

Mileage Tracker is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
